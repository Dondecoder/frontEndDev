DOM (Document Object Model) 
  The dom is a JavaScript representation of a webpage. 
  It's your JS "window" into the contents of a webpage.
  It's just a bunch of objects that you can interact with via JS. 
  
Docume object is our entry point into the world of the DOM. It contains representations of all the content on a page, plus tons of useful methods ad properties. It's created 
  automatically for us on the browser.
